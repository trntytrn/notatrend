GENERAL TIPS

- Cover up your face and wear clothes that don't reveal your identity

- Don't post where you're going anywhere and if you bring your phone turn off cellular data

- Don't take IDs or cards

- Carry a mixture of water and baking soda in a spray bottle in case of tear gas (milk can cause infections)
