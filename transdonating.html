DONATE TO BLACK TRANS LIVES

Black Trans Travel Fund
"Donated funds are redistributed directly to Black trans women in need, who can then have the autonomy to purchase private car ride services, purchase gas for their own vehicles, pay family or friends for a ride, or other alternatives of their choosing that best suit their comfort and needs."

For the Gworls - Medical Fund
"For The Gworls’ Emergency Medical Relief Medical Fund allows us to pay for Black trans folks to travel to clinics and pharmacies during this time, as well as provide co-pay assistance if needed so that they can continue to receive important prescriptions."

For the Gworls - Rent and Gender Affirming Surgery Fund
"With this fund, we actively fight to reduce homelessness rates in the Black transgender community, as well as lower the risk for affirmative surgeries being done in ways that put them at greater health risks."

The Okra Project
"The Okra Project is a collective that seeks to address the global crisis faced by Black Trans people by bringing home-cooked, healthy, and culturally specific meals and resources to Black Trans People wherever we can reach them."

Black Trans Femmes in the Arts
The mission of the BTFA Collective is to connect the community of black trans women and non-binary femmes in the arts & to build power among ourselves.

Black Trans Protestors Emergency Fund
"In partnership with Black Trans Travel Fund, For the Gworls, and the Okra Project, we are raising funds to support Black trans protestors with resources, medical care, and bail."

The Heavenly Angel Fund (PayPal) The Heavenly Angel Fund (CashApp)
The Heavenly Angel Fund (H.A.F) Project serves Black Transwomen in NYC during the Covid-19 crisis.

FEMMEPREMACY
FEMMEPREMACY is a safe party, workshop, and event space for queer, trans people of color that centers Black femmes.
